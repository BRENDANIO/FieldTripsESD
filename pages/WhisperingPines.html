Close-up of Destination

Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. Take Hwy 8 south to the Kamarno turnoff at Hwy 229.
  • 2. Turn right and follow this hwy past Road 14E.
  • 3. The farm is located on the right.
  • 4. Back into the property to unload students.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • Winnipeg Beach is a short drive away. You may also choose to return to Gimli.
  • Cautions
  • Back into the driveway off the Highway.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Food Options - Nothing available on site. Visit Winnipeg Beach or Gimli for food if required.
