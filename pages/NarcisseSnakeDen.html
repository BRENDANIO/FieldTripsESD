Directions for the Recommended Route

  • From Gimli
  • 1. Provincial Rd 231 West.
  • 2. Take 231 upto Fraserwood and right (north) at Hwy 7.
  • 3. Make a left back onto 231.
  • 4. Once you are at the Hwy 17 intersection turn north to the Snake Dens.
  • From Winnipeg Beach
  • 1. Take Hwy 229 to Hwy 17.
  • 2. Once you are at the Hwy 17 intersection turn north to the Snake Dens.
  • From Arborg
  • 1. Take Hwy 68 west to Hwy 17.
  • 2. Turn south onto Hwy 17.
  • From Riverton
  • 1. Take Hwy 8 south to 68.
  • 2. West on 68 to Hwy 17.
  • 3. Turn south onto Hwy 17.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • After unloading the students, move the bus into the designated parking area onsite.
  • Cautions
  • There is no available information on cautions at this time. This information will be updated as drivers provide input on what they recommend after handling this field trip.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Optional Route - Provincial Rd 231 which is a few minutes north of Fraserwood is shown on maps as the fastest route but is gravel and can be extremely dusty. Gimli drivers may choose to use Hwy 68 but this will add approx 20 minutes to the drive.
  • Food Options - It is recommended that you bring lunch and beverages with you. There are picnic tables around the park.
