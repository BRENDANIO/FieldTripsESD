Overview of Field Trip

Close-up of Destination

Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. Take Hwy 7 south to Winnipeg.
  • 2. Follow route 90 south under the Portage ave underpass.
  • 3. Keep to the right and turn right onto Tuxedo Blvd.
  • 4. Right onto Corydon ave.
  • 5. The zoo entrance will be on your right.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • The easiest parking will be on the east side of the lot.
  • Cautions
  • Railway crossing on route 90 near Saskatchewan Blvd (keep to your right).
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Optional Route - Follow the perimeter west around to Roblin Blvd. In this case the zoo will be positioned on your left. A turning lane is provided.
  • Entering the Zoo - Bus drivers are welcome to visit the Zoo at no cost. Inform the attendant you are the bus driver for the Evergreen group.
