Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. Take Hwy 8 south. Arborg drivers may choose to take Hwy 7 south.
  • 2. Take the perimeter east around to Pembina Hwy.
  • 3. Follow Pembina north a few Kms.
  • 4. Turn right onto Chancellor Matheson Drive at the U of M main entrance followed by a left at University Crescent.
  • 5. Take the first right onto Dysart Road and then immediately turn right onto Sidney Smith St.
  • 6. The drop off location is on your right.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • Can be easily found in that general area. A turn around will most likely be required on the service road a few meters ahead of the drop off spot.
  • Cautions
  • Several Railway crossing on the perimeter.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Food Options - Numerous restaurants on or near Pembina Hwy.
