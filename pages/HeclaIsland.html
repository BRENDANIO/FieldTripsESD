Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. From Hwy 8, head north directly to Hecla.
  • 2. Following Hwy 8 will lead all drivers to Hecla, directly into the park.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • Parking is available in most parts of the park.
  • Cautions
  • Village speed limit is 30km/h.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • This trip requires the bus to be operational during the entire visit. The driver must remain with the group for the entire trip as students will require relocation to different areas of the park.
  • An example of this was that the driver was required to drop half the students off at the fish plant, with the rest going to the tower. Shortly after, the driver needed to drive the fish plant group to the tower and the tower group down to the fish plant.
