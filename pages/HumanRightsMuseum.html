Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. Take Hwy 8 south. Arborg drivers may choose to take Hwy 7 south.
  • 2. Take the perimeter east around to Main St.
  • 3. Follow Main street through to York Ave.
  • 4. Once your past the Higgins underpass begin making your way into the left lane.
  • 5. Turn left onto York and then right at the lights onto Israel Asper Way.
  • 6. Use the roundabout to circle back to the front of the Museum.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • Confirm with the Tech center office that a parking location has been reserved. Parking is usually available directly across from the Shaw Park Ball Diamond. To gain access it is best to travel north on Israel Asper Way past the Ball Diamond and use the roundabout to circle back to the parking area.
  • Cautions
  • 1 Railway crossing on the perimeter.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • Not required for this trip.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Food Options - Numerous restaurants within the area.
