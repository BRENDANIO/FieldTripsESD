Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. Proceed onto Highway 8
  • 2. Continue on Highway 8 to Winnipeg
  • 3. Take the Perimeter West (MB-101 W)
  • 4. Turn left onto McGillivray Blvd
  • 5. Follow McGillivray Blvd until you find the Costco on your left.
