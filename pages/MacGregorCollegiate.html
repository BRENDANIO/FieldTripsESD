Directions for the Recommended Route

  • 1. GHS to Hwy 8.
  • 2. Take the Winnipeg perimeter around to Hwy #1.
  • 3. Follow Hwy #1 all the way to the McGregor turnoff #350.
  • The following route will set you up to easily unload students without them crossing over.
  • 4. Take 350 south and turn right onto Hampton St.
  • 5. Turn right at Fox St and the High School will be on your right hand side.
  • Alternate route into MacGregor. Requires students to cross over or the driver to perform a turn around.
  • 4. Turn onto 350 and take your first right onto the service road.
  • 5. Take a Left onto Fox st. The school will be on your left.

Additional Notes & Information

  • Parking
  • Is available across the street at the middle school.
  • Cautions
  • Railway crossings around the perimeter. Road work at Portage la Prairie bypass.
  • Fueling Station Options
  • If required, Esso in Headingly.
  • Extra Notes To Consider
  • Food Options - It is best to bring your own food and beverages. The number of food oulets are limited in this town. The school does have a small lunch room area with tables and chairs to sit and eat at.
